Webhook Setup

Teller sends webhooks for account disconnections and other events. To enable:

  1. Go to the Teller dashboard and navigate to your application settings
  2. Set the webhook URL to:
https://<your-domain>/webhooks/teller
  1. Copy the webhook signing secret from Teller and paste it in the Webhook Secret field above
  2. Breadbox verifies webhook signatures using this secret to ensure authenticity

Without webhooks, Breadbox relies on cron-based polling (every {{formatIntervalMinutes .SyncInterval}}) to detect changes.
