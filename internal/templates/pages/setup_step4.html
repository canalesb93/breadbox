Webhook URL (Optional)

Webhooks are optional. Without them, Breadbox will sync on its configured schedule. Webhooks allow near-real-time syncing when your bank pushes transaction updates.

To use webhooks, Breadbox must be reachable at a public HTTPS URL. The webhook endpoint your provider should call is: {your_base_url}/webhooks/plaid

Running locally or behind a firewall?

Cloudflare Tunnel is a free option that securely exposes your local Breadbox server to the internet without opening ports.
